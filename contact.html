Frequently Asked Questions

What is the mission of Bilqees?

Our mission is to provide authentic Islamic knowledge and resources to help Muslims strengthen their connection with Allah and deepen their understanding of the Quran, Hadith, and Islamic teachings.

Is the content on Bilqees accurate?

Yes, all content is compiled from authentic Islamic sources including the Quran, Sahih Hadith collections, and works of respected Islamic scholars. We prioritize accuracy and authenticity in all our materials.

Can I contribute content to Bilqees?

We welcome qualified contributions. Please contact us with details about your proposed content, expertise, and qualifications. All submissions will be reviewed for authenticity and alignment with our values.

Is Bilqees free to use?

Yes, all content on Bilqees is free and available to everyone. We believe Islamic knowledge should be accessible to all believers without any financial barriers.

How often is content updated?

We regularly add new content including daily verses, hadith, learning resources, and articles. New content is typically added several times per week.

Do you have an app for mobile devices?

Our website is fully responsive and works on all devices. We're currently developing dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android platforms.
