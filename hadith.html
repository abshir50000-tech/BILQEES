Daily Hadith

On Intention

Narrated by Umar ibn Al-Khattab

"The Messenger of Allah said: 'Verily, the deeds are only with the intention, and for every person is what he has intended. So whose migration was for Allah and His Messenger, then his migration was for Allah and His Messenger. And whose migration was for the world to gain something from it, or for a woman to marry her, then his migration was to what he migrated for.'"

Sahih Al-Bukhari, Book 1, Hadith 1

On Kindness and Mercy

Narrated by Abdur Rahman ibn Abdullah

"The Messenger of Allah said: 'The merciful will be shown mercy by Allah. Be merciful to those on earth, and the One in the heavens will be merciful to you.'"

Sunan At-Tirmidhi, Hadith 1924

On Knowledge

Narrated by Abu Hurairah

"The Messenger of Allah said: 'Whoever travels on a road in search of knowledge, Allah will cause him to travel on one of the roads of Paradise. Verily the angels submit themselves with their wings to the seeker of knowledge, for what he does is good.'"

Sunan At-Tirmidhi, Hadith 2646

On Good Character

Narrated by Abu Hurairah

"The Messenger of Allah said: 'The best of you are those who have the best manners and character.'"

Sahih Al-Bukhari, Book 76, Hadith 6

Understanding Hadith

Hadith are the recorded actions, sayings, and approvals of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). They form an essential part of Islamic jurisprudence and practice, providing practical examples of how to implement Quranic teachings in daily life.

Isnad (Chain of Narration)

The list of narrators who transmitted the hadith, ensuring authenticity and traceability back to the Prophet.

Matn (Text of Hadith)

The actual content or wording of the hadith that was reported by the narrators.

Authenticity Levels

Hadith are classified as Sahih (authentic), Hasan (good), or Daif (weak) based on the strength of their chain and text.
