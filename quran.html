Why Read the Quran?

🌟 Guidance

The Quran provides clear guidance for all aspects of life and leads to success in this world and the Hereafter.

🧘 Peace

Reading and reflecting on the Quran brings tranquility and peace to the heart and soul.

📚 Knowledge

The Quran contains wisdom, legal rulings, stories of the prophets, and understanding of the Divine.

💪 Strength

The Quran strengthens faith, increases remembrance of Allah, and provides spiritual fortitude.
